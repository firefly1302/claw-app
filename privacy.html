Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

Claw ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI assistant app for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

When you use Claw, we process the following data:

  • Account information: Email address for authentication.
  • Conversations: Messages you send to the AI assistant are processed to generate responses. Conversations are stored on your personal server instance.
  • Usage data: Basic usage metrics (message count, timestamps) for service limits and billing.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your conversations for advertising.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not share your conversations with other users.

How Your Data Is Processed

Your messages are sent to AI model providers (such as Anthropic or OpenAI) to generate responses. These providers process your messages according to their respective privacy policies and data processing agreements. Messages are not used to train AI models.

Data Storage & Security

Your conversation data is stored on isolated, encrypted server instances hosted in European data centers. Each user gets their own dedicated instance. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

BYOS (Bring Your Own Server)

Claw supports connecting to your own self-hosted server. In this mode, no conversation data passes through our infrastructure at all. You are fully in control.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing

Children's Privacy

Claw is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@claw-app.com
